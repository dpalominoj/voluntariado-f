Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use Volunteer Connect.

To sign up as a volunteer, you'll first need to create an account. Once registered, you can browse available programs and activities and sign up for those that interest you.

If you are an organizer, you will need an 'Organizer' account. Once approved, you will have access to a dashboard where you can create and manage your programs and activities.

For any issues or questions not covered here, please reach out to our support team at support@volunteerconnect.example.com.
